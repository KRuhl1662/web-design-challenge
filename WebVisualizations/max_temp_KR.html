Max Temperature

As expected the weather becomes increasingly warmer as one approaches the equator. (O Deg Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This is due to the tilt of the earth at this time of year.
